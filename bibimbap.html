Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap is a tradtional Korean dish and it is served as a warm bowl of Recipes topped with sauteed vegetables and chili pepper paste. Meat and egg are common additions

Ingredients

Meat and meat sauce

  • 100g beef mince
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tbs mionced garlic

Vegetables and others

  • 250g seasoned spinach
  • 350g seasoned bean sprouts
  • 100g shiitake mushroom
  • 120g carrots
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 to 4 serving portions of steamed rice
  • 3 to 4 eggs
  • Some toasted seasoned seaweed, shredded
  • Some cooking oil

Bibimbap Sauce

  • 2 Tbsp gochujang
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 Tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp vinegar (apple vinegar is good)
  • 1 tsp minced garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare and cook ingredients as below

    2. For meat, mix the beef mince with the meat sauce listed above. Marinate the meat for about 30 mins while you are working on other ingredients to enhance the flavour. Add some cooking oil into a wok and cook the meat on medium high to high heat. It takes about 3 to 5 mins to thoroughly cook it.

  2. Mix the bibimbap sauce ingredients in a bowl
  3. Cook spinach and bean sprouts per linked recipe
  4. Rinse, peel and juilenne the carrots. Add some cooking oil and 1/4 tsp of fine sea salt in a wok and cook the carrots on medium high to high heat for 2 to 3 mins
  5. Clean/rinse the shiitake mushrooms and thinly slice them. Add some cooking oil and 1/4 tsp of fine sea salt in a wok and cook the mushrooms on medium high to high heat until they are all cooked. (It takes 2 to 3 mins.)
  6. Make fried eggs
  7. put the rice into a bowl and add the meat, assorted vegetables, seasoned seaweed, bibimbap sauce, and the egg on top of the rice
  8. Mix ingredients and enjoy
